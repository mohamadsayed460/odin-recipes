Heaven on Earth Cake

A delicious no-bake dessert

This delightful Heaven on Earth Cake features layers of airy angel food cake, sweet cherry pie filling, creamy vanilla pudding, and fluffy whipped topping, garnished with sliced almonds.

The cake is perfect for summer gatherings or any occasion that calls for a refreshing and indulgent treat, sure to impress your guests with its beautiful presentation and irresistible flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (10-ounce) prepared angel food cake (store-bought or homemade from a mix, baked and cooled)
  • 1 (21-ounce) can cherry pie filling
  • 1 (3.4-ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 (8-ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed (or use fresh whipped cream)
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds, toasted (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the angel food cake according to the package instructions. Once baked, allow it to cool completely, then cut it into 1-inch cubes.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the instant vanilla pudding mix and cold milk until the mixture thickens, about 2 minutes. Let it sit for a few minutes to fully set.
  3. In a large trifle bowl or a deep glass dish, start layering the cake. Begin with a layer of angel food cake cubes at the bottom.
  4. Spread a layer of cherry pie filling over the cake cubes.
  5. Next, add a layer of the prepared vanilla pudding over the cherry pie filling.
  6. Spread a layer of whipped topping over the pudding layer.
  7. Repeat the layering process (cake, cherry pie filling, pudding, whipped topping) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of whipped topping on top.
  8. Garnish the top of the cake with toasted sliced almonds for added texture and flavor, if desired.
  9. Cover the cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Before serving, you can add extra cherry pie filling on top for garnish if desired. Serve chilled and enjoy!
